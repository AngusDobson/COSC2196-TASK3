SKILLS AND JOBS

Below are the positions that our team is looking for to take the Dinnerizer project to the next phase.

SENIOR SOFTWARE ENGINEER – BACKEND

The Senior Software Engineer will be responsible for leading the backend development of the Dinnerizer app. They should have a strong background in software development and good expertise in building backend systems that are scalable. They need to have great problem-solving skills and an innovative mind as they will be essential in designing and implementing different features that will drive the meal planning and grocery shopping experience to the next level. They will need good teamwork skills to collaborate closely with the rest of the team to ensure that integration with APIs is smooth.

Skills and Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or a related field.
  • Experience in backend development using languages such as Python, Java or C#
  • A good understanding of databases and how to model data for efficient storage
  • Experience with cloud platforms like AWS
  • Strong problem-solving skills with a track record of delivering high-quality code on schedule.

UX/UI DESIGNER

The UX/UI Designer will be primarily responsible in ensuring that the Dinnerizer app offers an intuitive and comfortable user experience. They will work closely with the development team and product managers to gather user requirements and turn them into design solutions. Their creative skills will be crucial in creating a visually appealing interface which will flow well and promote user engagement.

Skills and Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in design or a related field.
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Adobe XD or similar.
  • Strong understanding of user-centered design principles
  • Experience in creating wireframes and mockups for both mobile and web applications
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment
  • A portfolio which showcases previous UI/UX design projects

DATA SCIENTIST

The Data Scientist will play a crucial role in using data to enhance the functionality and personalization of the Dinnerizer app. Their expertise in data analysis will be used to understand user preferences and behaviours to recommend personalized meal options. Additionally, they will optimize the grocery shopping list generation to minimize waste and improve the efficiency of it.

Skills and Qualifications:

  • Masters in data science, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • Proficiency in data analysis and visualization using tools like Python.
  • Experience with recommendation systems and natural language processing
  • Strong communication skills to present complex data to non-technical stakeholders
  • Able to work with large datasets and extract valuable insights to drive improvements in the product.

QA ENGINEER

The QA Engineer will be responsible for ensuring the quality and reliability of the Dinnerizer application. They will work very closely with the development team to design and execute test plans, identify and report problems, and ensure the application is meeting a high-quality standard before each release. Their attention to detail and passion for UX will be important in maintaining a great reputation.

Skills and Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
  • Familiar with testing frameworks like JUnit or similar.
  • Ability to create and maintain test scripts and cases.
  • Strong problem-solving skills to identify and present issues
  • Proven experience with software testing
  • Great communication skills to collaborate with the development team and report results of tests
