SUMMARY

Throughout the transition from Assignment 2 to 3, the Gravy Seals team demonstrated a great ability to adapt and collaborate, even with geographical and time differences. The group hit the ground running, realizing the swiftness with which project timelines can progress. While there were challenges in synchronizing team meetings and balancing individual priorities, the foundation built during Assignment 2 bolstered the group's trust and ensured that they met their objectives. A significant highlight was the team's early initiative to set up the website, allowing simultaneous enhancement alongside the project report. Consistent communication emerged as a critical component for the group, ensuring clarity and fostering a positive work environment. Regular meetings, both scheduled and informal, coupled with tools like GitHub and MS Teams, facilitated transparency and efficiency. Despite the occasional challenges, the Gravy Seals team maintained their productivity and camaraderie, making the collaborative experience rewarding for all members.
